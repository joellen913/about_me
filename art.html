I have always found art as an expressive medium that could be used
to portray feelings, emotions, sentiment, and passion. Since I was a
child, art has always found its way into my life, whether it was
from photographs to pieces in a museum. About five years ago,
my art teacher introduced me to Adobe illustrator and photoshop,
and as of two years ago, I began to work with procreate on my
sister's iPad, which is when I started my digital art
era. I constantly find myself painting and digitally
sketching landscapes, fruit, or buildings; painting
people is not my strong suit.
